# CAM License Exam Study Guide

A comprehensive study system for the **Florida Community Association Manager (CAM)** license exam and the **CMCA** (Certified Manager of Community Associations) national certification.

## Exam Domains Covered

1. **Governance & Compliance (23%)** — Statutes, governing documents, board duties, CAM licensing
2. **Financial Management (22%)** — Budgets, assessments, reserves, SIRS, financial reporting
3. **Risk Management (12%)** — Insurance, structural integrity, milestone inspections
4. **Meetings & Events (18%)** — Notice requirements, quorum, voting, elections, proxies
5. **Property Maintenance (14%)** — Common elements, preventive maintenance, vendor management
6. **Contracting (11%)** — Management contracts, competitive bidding, contract types

## SMS Study Program

The SMS program uses **spaced repetition** (Leitner system) to move concepts from short-term to long-term memory over 30 days:

- **90 total messages** (3/day: morning, midday, evening)
- Each concept appears **5 times** at increasing intervals (Day 1 → +1 → +3 → +7 → +14)
- Questions are **reworded** each repetition to deepen understanding
- Built-in **mnemonics**, **trap alerts**, and **confidence markers**
- `sms_schedule.json` is ready to plug into any SMS automation platform
