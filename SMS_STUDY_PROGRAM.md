# CAM Exam - 30-Day SMS Study Program

## HOW THIS PROGRAM WORKS

### Sending Schedule
- **3 questions per day** (Morning, Midday, Evening)
- **Send the QUESTION first**, then the **ANSWER 15-30 minutes later**
- Total: **90 question slots** across 30 days

### Spaced Repetition Built In
Every concept appears **multiple times** at increasing intervals based on the Leitner system:
- **First exposure:** Day N (new concept introduced)
- **2nd exposure:** Day N+1 (next day reinforcement)
- **3rd exposure:** Day N+3 (short-term recall)
- **4th exposure:** Day N+7 (medium-term recall)
- **5th exposure:** Day N+14 or later (long-term lock-in)

Questions are tagged with `[NEW]`, `[REP-2]`, `[REP-3]`, `[REP-4]`, `[REP-5]` so you know where each falls in the repetition cycle. Repeated questions are **reworded** each time to deepen understanding rather than just pattern-match.

### Built-In Learning Strategies
- **Mnemonics** embedded directly in answers (marked with üß†)
- **"Why it matters"** context in answers to build understanding, not just memorization
- **Trick alerts** flagged with ‚ö†Ô∏è for commonly missed concepts
- **Confidence builders** ‚Äî easy wins mixed in with harder material
- **Interleaving** ‚Äî domains are mixed within each day, not siloed

### SMS Format
Each message fits in ~160-300 characters (1-2 SMS segments). Answers are slightly longer to include the memory hook.
