## TOP 10 EXAM TRAPS

1. **"Developer has no fiduciary duty"** → FALSE. Developer has SAME duties.
2. **"CAM license required for all associations"** → FALSE. Only when budget exceeds $100,000.
3. **"Proxies allowed for condo board elections"** → FALSE. Secret ballot only.
4. **"SIRS reserves can be waived by vote"** → FALSE. Cannot be waived (post-2025).
5. **"DBPR regulates HOAs"** → FALSE. DBPR regulates condos, co-ops, timeshares. NOT HOAs.
6. **"Commingling funds is okay temporarily"** → FALSE. NEVER commingle.
7. **"The CAM can give legal advice"** → FALSE. CAM is not an attorney.
8. **"Board meeting quorum = 30%"** → FALSE. 30% is for MEMBER meetings. Board = majority of directors.
9. **"Recall needs majority of those present"** → FALSE. Majority of ALL voting interests.
10. **"Records belong to the management company"** → FALSE. Records ALWAYS belong to the association.
